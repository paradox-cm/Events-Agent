About Reno Family Events

The Reno Family Events web app displays family and teen-friendly events collected from 20+ local sources in the Reno, Sparks, Carson City, and Lake Tahoe areas.

How It Works

Our automated agent scans local event sources daily, filters for family-friendly content, and presents the events in an easy-to-browse format. Events are grouped by date and include all the details you need to plan your family activities.

Family-Friendly Filtering

Events are automatically filtered to exclude adult-only venues and activities. We focus on events that are suitable for families with children and teenagers.

Real-Time Updates

Click the "Refresh" button to collect the latest events from all sources. The agent respects website terms of service and uses polite delays between requests.

Email Digest

The agent can also send daily email digests with new events. Configure your email settings in the agent's configuration file.

Tip: Use the timeframe filters (Today, This Week, Next Week, All Events) to focus on events that fit your schedule.
